How To Play




Choose Your Hero!


A player needs someone to have something, so decide who you
want to main first! This decision-making skill will be important
when you reach Epic in Mobile Legends (Ranked Game).

Be wary of your laning


In the game, you will have the side lanes formatted for the first
3:40 minutes of the game. The EXP lane will level your hero quicker,
and the Gold lane will let you earn more gold for your equipment.

Push your lane!

Since the goal of the game is to destroy the enemy's base, you
should destroy the turrets that are blocking your way. All turrets
have a shield on part of the game, which will make it harder but
can be helpful in any given situation.

Know how to gank.

Ganking is a way to join team fights safely and suddenly for the
enemies. To gank, go to a bush where a fight is occurring. You
should then use a skill that can suddenly change the tides of the battle.

Communicate!

Since teamwork relies on help, tell them if you want them as a
backup or to retreat when needed. These controls can be seen at
the top-right corner.

Decide on the Lord

Within 9 minutes of the game, the Sanctuary Lord will spawn in
the game. The Lord can be helpful and let you push for the team.

Push for the win!

If you manage to wipe your enemy team out, don't go for the creeps
right away! Try following your minions and attack the turrets.

Upgrade your Emblems

Emblems are light tune-ups for a hero and can provide you with quick
assistance! Try focusing on grabbing Magic Dust from certain events
or emblems from achievements.

Do other game modes

Once you have the hang of Classic mode, try other game modes that
can enhance your experience! If you want a quick game, use Brawl
Mode. If you want to discover a fun chess experience, play Magic Chess.

Buy items in the shop

The shop is a great way to increase your collection of heroes and
skins. Once you have the hang of newer heroes, try getting another one.
